System Overview

The Bugasan ni Mayang Inventory System is designed to help rice retailers efficiently manage their inventory, track sales, and streamline operations.

Main Objective

To develop a comprehensive system that manages products, inventory levels, supplier information, and customer orders while ensuring stock availability and preventing shortages.

Inventory Management

Track stock levels, set reorder points, and receive alerts when inventory is running low.

Supplier Management

Maintain supplier information, track purchase orders, and manage supplier relationships.

Sales Tracking

Record sales transactions, generate invoices, and analyze sales performance over time.

Reporting & Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports on inventory, sales, and profitability to make data-driven decisions.

System Features
